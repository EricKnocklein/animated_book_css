In the Light of Golden Eyes

E.K. Littlehill

By the time he had returned to the cathedral, it was dark. In the darkness, he was convinced that the hill he had climbed in this passage to the mountains had moved closer to the cathedral. But before he could verify this notion, he heard chanting coming from the front of the great stone cave. This was no chanting of the priests, so the man knew that the robed figures had returned.
